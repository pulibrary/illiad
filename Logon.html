Forgot Password? | First Time Users

Existing Users

If your ILS account username is the same as your Princeton Net ID account, please login to the system with your Princeton Net ID Account Password. If your ILS account username is different than your Princeton Net ID or if you don't have a Princeton Net ID, please login to the system with the same credentials you've used before.

New Users

If your are a Princeton Net ID account holder, please login to the system with your Princeton Net ID Account Password. You will be taken to a brief registration form that will ask you to fill in your campus contact details. If you do not have a Princeton Net ID account use the ILS self-registration form. If you have any questions about these changes, or problems logging into the system, please email [ilsborr@princeton.edu] at any time or call [609-258-3272] during regular office hours.

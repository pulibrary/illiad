Existing Users

If your ILS account username is the same as your Princeton Net ID account you will now login to the system with your Princeton Net ID Account Password. If your ILS account username is different than your Princeton Net ID or you don't have a Princeton Net ID you will login to the system you login with the same credentials you've used before.

New Users

If your are a Princeton Net ID account holder login to the system with your Princeton Net ID Account Password. You will be taken to a brief registration form that will ask you to fill in your campus contact details. If you do not have a Princeton Net ID account use the ILS self-registration form. If you have any questions about these changes or problems logging into the system contact ILS services via email [ilsborr@princeton.edu] at anytime or via phone [609-258-3272] during the office's business hours.

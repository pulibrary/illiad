Information related to COVID-19

We are accepting Article Express request and will attempt to fulfill them as Library Staff and Partner Library materials are available during the campus closure. Requests will likely take far longer to fill than during times of normal operation.

Interlibrary Loan Requests for physical items will not be fulfilled during the Library's closure through 4/30. For remote support, contact us via our Ask Us service or contact a subject librarian. More information is available at PUL SUPPORT FOR REMOTE RESEARCH, TEACHING, & LEARNING.
